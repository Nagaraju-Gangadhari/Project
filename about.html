ABOUT US

Export and import companies play a crucial role in international trade by facilitating the movement of goods and services across borders. These companies are involved in buying and selling products and services between different countries.

We Are HANUMAN INTERNATIONAL

KEY ROLES IN INTERNATIONAL TRADE

Exporters: Companies that sell products or services to customers in other countries.

Importers: Companies that buy products or services from foreign suppliers for use or resale in their home country.

Freight Forwarders: Companies that arrange transportation and logistics for the movement of goods across borders.

Customs Brokers: Individuals or firms that assist in clearing goods through customs and ensuring compliance with regulations.

Trade Finance Providers: Financial institutions that offer services such as letters of credit to facilitate international transactions.

Trade Documents

Invoice: A document specifying the details of the transaction, including prices, quantities, and terms.

Bill of Lading: A document issued by a carrier acknowledging receipt of goods for shipment.

Certificate of Origin: A document certifying the origin of the goods.

Customs Declaration: Documentation required by customs authorities detailing the nature, value, and origin of the goods.
